<p><p>The explosion-proof mobile phone market is categorized by two primary certification types: ATEX and IECEx. ATEX certification is established by the European Union, ensuring devices are safe for use in potentially explosive atmospheres, often found in industrial settings. IECEx certification, recognized internationally, provides assurance that equipment meets safety standards for explosive environments, facilitating global trade. Both certifications are crucial for ensuring the reliability and safety of mobile phones used in hazardous locations, such as oil and gas sectors.</p></p>
