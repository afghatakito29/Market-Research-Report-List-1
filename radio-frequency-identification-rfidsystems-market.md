<p><ul><li>Passive RFID System</li><li>Active RFID System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RFID systems are categorized into two primary market types: passive and active RFID systems. Passive RFID systems consist of tags that do not have their own power source; they rely on the electromagnetic field generated by the reader to transmit data. These are typically cost-effective and suitable for inventory management. In contrast, active RFID systems come with their own power source, allowing for longer read ranges and real-time tracking capabilities, making them ideal for asset tracking and logistics applications.</p></p>
