<p><p>The Serration Balloon Catheter market is segmented into two primary types: Over-the-Wire (OTW) Serration Balloon Catheters and Rapid Exchange Serration Balloon Catheters. OTW Serration Balloon Catheters are characterized by their design that allows the catheter to be advanced over a guide wire, providing enhanced maneuverability and control during procedures. In contrast, Rapid Exchange Serration Balloon Catheters enable quick catheter exchanges, offering efficiency and convenience in minimally invasive interventions. Both types cater to different clinical needs and preferences in balloon angioplasty and other vascular treatments.</p></p>
