<p><p>Cloud-based POS systems encompass three main market types. Installed-Mobile software is designed for mobile devices, offering flexibility for businesses that operate on the go but still requiring local installation. In contrast, cloud-based software leverages internet connectivity, enabling real-time data access, automatic updates, and remote management, making it ideal for businesses looking for scalability and ease of use.</p></p>
