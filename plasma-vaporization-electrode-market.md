<p><p>The Plasma Vaporization Electrode market is segmented into bipolar and monopolar types, each serving distinct applications in medical and industrial fields. Bipolar electrodes utilize two conductors to create a localized effect, making them ideal for procedures requiring precision and minimal thermal spread. In contrast, monopolar electrodes use a single active electrode and return pad, generating wider energy distribution, which is useful for larger tissue ablation. Both types offer unique advantages depending on the specific requirements of surgical or cutting applications.</p></p>
