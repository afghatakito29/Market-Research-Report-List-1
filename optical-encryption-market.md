<p><strong>The Optical Encryption Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>OTN or Layer 1</li><li>MACsec or Layer 2</li><li>IPsec or Layer 3</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical encryption market consists of three primary layers: OTN (Optical Transport Network) or Layer 1 focuses on securing data at the physical layer, ensuring robust protection during transmission across fiber networks. MACsec (Media Access Control Security) or Layer 2 operates at the data link layer, providing encryption for Ethernet frames, enhancing security in local area networks. IPsec (Internet Protocol Security) or Layer 3 encrypts data at the network layer, safeguarding IP packets for secure communication over the internet, making it essential for secure data transfer.</p></p>
