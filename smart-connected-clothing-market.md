<p><p>The Smart Connected Clothing market encompasses various connectivity types that enhance garment functionality. Wi-Fi enables direct internet access for data sharing and app integration. Bluetooth facilitates short-range communication with smartphones and devices for tracking health metrics. Near Field Communication (NFC) allows for tap-based interactions, enhancing user convenience. Infrared (IR) aids in remote control features or data transmission in close proximity. Motion sensors detect physical activity and gestures, while other technologies may include GPS or RFID, broadening smart clothing capabilities.</p></p>
