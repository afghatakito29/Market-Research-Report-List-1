<p><strong>The Flame Retardant Polyester Fiber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inherent FRP Staple Fiber</li><li>Treated FRP Staple Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flame retardant polyester fiber market comprises two main types: inherent flame-retardant polyester (FRP) staple fiber and treated FRP staple fiber. Inherent FRP staple fiber is manufactured with built-in flame retardant properties, making it effective against fire without additional treatment. In contrast, treated FRP staple fiber undergoes chemical processing to enhance its fire-resistant capabilities. Both types are widely used in textiles, construction, automotive, and industrial applications, providing safety and compliance with fire regulations while maintaining functionality and comfort.</p></p>
