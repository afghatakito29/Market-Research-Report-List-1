<p><p>The float sensor market comprises two primary types: top-mounted and side-mounted sensors. Top-mounted float sensors are installed from the top of the tank and are ideal for applications with limited access, offering easy installation and maintenance. Side-mounted float sensors are fixed on the side of the tank, suitable for larger tanks and providing greater accuracy in level measurement. Both types serve industries like water treatment, HVAC, and petrochemical, ensuring reliable liquid level monitoring in various settings.</p></p>
