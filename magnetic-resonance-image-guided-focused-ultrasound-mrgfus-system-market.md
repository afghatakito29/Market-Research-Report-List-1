<p><p>The Magnetic Resonance Image-Guided Focused Ultrasound (MRgFUS) system market is classified into three main types: extracorporeal devices, intracavitary devices, and interstitial devices. Extracorporeal devices apply focused ultrasound from outside the body, ideal for treating larger, superficial lesions. Intracavitary devices are inserted into body cavities, allowing targeted treatment of internal conditions. Interstitial devices involve placing ultrasound sources directly within tissues, providing precise, localized treatment for tumors and other conditions. Each type offers unique applications in non-invasive therapies.</p></p>
