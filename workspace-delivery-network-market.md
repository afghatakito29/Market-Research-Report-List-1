<p><p>The Workspace Delivery Network market comprises Traditional WAN and SD-WAN solutions. Traditional WAN relies on dedicated, circuit-based connections, offering stable but costly networking options primarily for enterprises with predictable bandwidth needs. Conversely, SD-WAN utilizes a software-based approach to dynamically manage network traffic across multiple connections, enhancing flexibility, cost-efficiency, and performance, particularly for cloud-based applications. This adaptability makes SD-WAN increasingly popular in modern workplaces, promoting seamless connectivity and improved user experiences in diverse environments.</p></p>
