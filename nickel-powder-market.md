<p><strong>The Nickel Powder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Carbonyl Nickel Powder</li><li>Electrolytic Nickel Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nickel powder market is primarily divided into two types: carbonyl nickel powder and electrolytic nickel powder. Carbonyl nickel powder is produced through the chemical process of nickel carbonyl, resulting in ultra-fine particles with high purity, and is widely used in applications like batteries and catalysts. Electrolytic nickel powder, on the other hand, is derived from the electrolysis of nickel salts, offering a coarser texture suitable for applications such as sintering and manufacturing of magnetic materials. Both types are crucial for various industrial applications.</p></p>
