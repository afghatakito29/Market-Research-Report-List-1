<p><strong>The Natural Cork Flooring Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Adhesive Cork Floor"</li><li>"Locking Cork Floor"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Natural Cork Flooring market features two primary types: Adhesive Cork Floor and Locking Cork Floor. Adhesive Cork Floors are installed using a glue-down method, ensuring a strong bond with the subfloor, which enhances stability and minimizes movement. In contrast, Locking Cork Floors utilize a tongue-and-groove system for easy installation without adhesives, allowing for floating floor designs. Both types offer sustainable benefits, sound insulation, and comfort, making them popular choices for eco-conscious consumers looking for stylish flooring solutions.</p></p>
