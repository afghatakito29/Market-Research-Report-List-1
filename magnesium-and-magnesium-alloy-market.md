<p><p>The magnesium and magnesium alloy market is primarily categorized into two types: cast alloys and wrought alloys. Cast alloys are produced by pouring molten magnesium into molds, resulting in complex shapes and high strength, often used in automotive and aerospace applications. Wrought alloys, on the other hand, are processed through methods like rolling and forging, offering improved mechanical properties and ductility, making them suitable for structural components. Both types are essential for lightweight applications in various industries, promoting efficiency and performance.</p></p>
