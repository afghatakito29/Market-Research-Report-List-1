<p><ul><li>Direct Current Multimeter</li><li>Alternating Current Multimeter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The benchtop multimeter market comprises two primary types: Direct Current (DC) multimeters and Alternating Current (AC) multimeters. DC multimeters are designed to measure voltage, current, and resistance in circuits with a constant flow of electricity, making them essential for battery and electronic diagnostics. In contrast, AC multimeters measure voltage, current, and frequency in circuits where the current changes direction periodically, suitable for assessing household and industrial power systems. Both types serve critical roles in various electronic testing and engineering applications.</p></p>
