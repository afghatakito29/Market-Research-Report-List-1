<p><p>The SUV steel wheel market comprises three primary types: casting, forging, and others. Casting involves pouring molten steel into molds, creating complex shapes with good surface finish, commonly used for mass production. Forging entails shaping steel under heat and pressure for enhanced strength and durability, often preferred for high-performance vehicles. Other methods may include roll forming or stamped wheels, which offer varied designs and functionalities. Together, these types cater to diverse consumer needs and vehicle specifications in the SUV segment.</p></p>
