<p><strong>The Molecular Blood Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PCR</li><li>PCR-RFLP</li><li>AS-PCR or PCR-SSP</li><li>Multiplex PCR</li><li>Real Time PCR</li><li>Sanger DNA Sequencing</li><li>Pyrosequencing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The molecular blood market encompasses various diagnostic techniques, each serving distinct purposes. PCR (Polymerase Chain Reaction) amplifies DNA for analysis. PCR-RFLP (Restriction Fragment Length Polymorphism) assesses genetic diversity through fragment variations. AS-PCR (Allele-Specific PCR) detects specific alleles, while PCR-SSP (PCR-Specific Probe) identifies single nucleotide polymorphisms. Multiplex PCR enables simultaneous amplification of multiple targets. Real-Time PCR provides quantification during amplification. Sanger DNA Sequencing delivers high accuracy in determining DNA sequences, while Pyrosequencing offers rapid sequence analysis through real-time detection.</p></p>
