<p><p>The vitrified clay pipes market comprises various types, primarily Open Trench Socket Pipes and Microtunneling (Jacking) Vitrified Clay Pipes. Open Trench Socket Pipes are traditionally installed using trenching methods, allowing for easy access and connection of the pipes. Conversely, Microtunneling Vitrified Clay Pipes utilize advanced, trenchless technology for installation, minimizing surface disruption and reducing construction time. Both types offer durability, resistance to chemical corrosion, and are environmentally friendly, making them popular choices in sewage and drainage systems.</p></p>
