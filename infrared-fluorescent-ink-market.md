<p><strong>The Infrared Fluorescent Ink Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Infrared Excited Ink</li><li>Infrared Absorption Ink</li><li>Infrared Covering Ink</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The infrared fluorescent ink market comprises various types tailored for specific applications. Infrared excited ink emits fluorescence when exposed to infrared light, making it suitable for security and authentication purposes. Infrared absorption ink, on the other hand, absorbs infrared wavelengths and is often used in thermal imaging and concealed printing. Infrared covering ink provides a layer of protection and obscurity, utilized for privacy or anti-counterfeiting measures. Each type serves unique needs in security and innovation across industries.</p></p>
