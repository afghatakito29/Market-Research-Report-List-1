<p><p>The ozone generator market for hospitals encompasses three main types: High Voltage Discharge Type, UV Irradiation Type, and Electrolysis Type. High Voltage Discharge Type generators create ozone by applying high voltage to oxygen, suitable for air and water treatment. UV Irradiation Type uses ultraviolet light to generate ozone from oxygen, effective for surface sterilization. Electrolysis Type involves passing electric current through water to produce ozone, often used for disinfecting medical equipment. Each type serves crucial roles in infection control and air purification.</p></p>
