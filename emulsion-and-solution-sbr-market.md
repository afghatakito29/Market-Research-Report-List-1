<p><p>The Emulsion and Solution Styrene-Butadiene Rubber (SBR) markets encompass two distinct types of synthetic rubber. In contrast, Solution SBR (s-SBR) is synthesized via a solution polymerization technique, offering superior performance characteristics, such as lower rolling resistance and improved wet traction, making it ideal for high-performance tires and automotive applications.</p></p>
