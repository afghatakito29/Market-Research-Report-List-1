<p><p>The children's cycling helmet market features three main types: normal half helmets, plum helmets, and full helmets. Normal half helmets offer basic protection, covering only the top and back of the head, making them lightweight and comfortable for young riders. Plum helmets provide enhanced safety with a slightly expanded design that covers more of the head while still allowing for ventilation. Full helmets offer the most protection, enclosing the entire head and providing secure fit, ideal for more adventurous cycling activities.</p></p>
