<p><p>The medical beds and chairs market is categorized into three main types: manual, semi-electric, and electric. Manual beds and chairs require manual adjustments for positioning, offering simplicity and lower costs. Semi-electric options combine manual adjustments for height with electric controls for back and leg positioning, providing a balance of convenience and affordability. Electric beds and chairs are fully powered, allowing users to adjust all settings with ease, enhancing comfort and accessibility, particularly for healthcare settings where patient care is paramount.</p></p>
