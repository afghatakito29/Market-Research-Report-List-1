<p><ul><li>Thermal Magnetic MCCB</li><li>Electronic Trip MCCB</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The DC MCCB (Moulded Case Circuit Breaker) market is primarily segmented into two types: Thermal Magnetic MCCBs and Electronic Trip MCCBs. Thermal Magnetic MCCBs operate using bimetallic strips for overload protection and electromagnetic mechanisms for short circuits, providing reliable and straightforward performance. In contrast, Electronic Trip MCCBs use advanced electronic sensors for precise fault detection and adjustable trip settings, offering enhanced customization and improved response times. Both types cater to different application needs in electrical distribution systems, ensuring safety and efficiency.</p></p>
