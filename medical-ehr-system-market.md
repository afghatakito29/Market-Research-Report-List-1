<p><p>The medical Electronic Health Record (EHR) system market is primarily categorized into two types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based EHR systems allow healthcare providers to store and access patient data over the internet, offering scalability, lower upfront costs, and remote accessibility. In contrast, on-premise EHR systems are installed locally on hospital servers, providing greater control over data security and customization but typically requiring higher initial investments and ongoing maintenance. Both types aim to enhance patient care and operational efficiency.</p></p>
