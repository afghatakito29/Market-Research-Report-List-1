<p><p>The pharmaceutical industry isolator system market includes two main types: sterility test isolators and sterility production isolators. Sterility test isolators are designed to provide a controlled environment for testing the sterility of pharmaceutical products, ensuring that contaminants are minimized during testing. In contrast, sterility production isolators are utilized in the actual manufacturing process, allowing aseptic operations to occur without exposure to environmental contaminants. Both systems are vital for maintaining product integrity and regulatory compliance in drug manufacturing.</p></p>
