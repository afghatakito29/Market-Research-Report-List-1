<p><p>The Black Tea Extract market is divided into two main types: hot water soluble and cold water soluble extracts. Hot water soluble extracts are primarily obtained by brewing tea leaves with hot water, making them ideal for products requiring brewing, such as beverages and instant teas. Cold water soluble extracts, on the other hand, dissolve in cold water and are used in ready-to-drink products, dietary supplements, and functional food applications, catering to consumers seeking convenience and versatility in consumption.</p></p>
