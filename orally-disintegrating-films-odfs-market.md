They come in two main types: orodispersible films, which are intended for oral administration to deliver medications quickly and conveniently, and oromucosal films, which are specifically formulated for absorption through the oral mucosa, allowing for faster onset of action. Both types cater to patients who may have difficulty swallowing tablets or prefer a discreet, portable option for medication delivery.</p></p>
