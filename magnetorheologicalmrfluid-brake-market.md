<p><p>The Magnetorheological (MR) Fluid Brake market can be categorized into two main types: piston and rotary. Piston-type MR brakes utilize a piston mechanism to engage and disengage braking forces using magnetorheological fluid, providing precise control and response. Conversely, rotary MR brakes operate on a rotating system, where the fluid's viscosity is altered to manage torque and braking efficiency dynamically. Both types leverage the unique properties of MR fluids for adaptive performance and enhanced safety in various applications, including automotive and industrial machinery.</p></p>
