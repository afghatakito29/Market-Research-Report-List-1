<p><ul><li>Point Sensors</li><li>Multipoint Sensors</li><li>3D Line Confocal Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Confocal sensor systems are categorized into three main types: point sensors, multipoint sensors, and 3D line confocal sensors. Point sensors measure single points with high precision, ideal for applications requiring detailed surface analysis. Multipoint sensors collect data from multiple points simultaneously, enhancing efficiency in inspecting larger areas. 3D line confocal sensors capture continuous profiles across a line, allowing for rapid 3D surface mapping. Together, these technologies enhance measurement accuracy and speed across various industrial and scientific applications.</p></p>
