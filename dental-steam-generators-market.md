<p><ul><li>Integral Boiler</li><li>Remote Boiler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental steam generators market includes two primary types: integral boilers and remote boilers. Integral boilers are compact units integrated directly into dental equipment, offering convenience and space efficiency for dental practices. In contrast, remote boilers are separate units located away from the treatment area, providing high steam output and reliability for larger facilities. Both types cater to different operational needs, with integral boilers suited for smaller setups and remote boilers ideal for larger clinics with extensive sterilization requirements.</p></p>
